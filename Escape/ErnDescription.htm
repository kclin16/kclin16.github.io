Scented Wax Melter

At first, I didn't know what this was called, but now that I just looked under the thing at the label, I notice that it is called a Diffuser. What it does is it spreads a scent for aromatherapy purposes. To set it up, one places a chunk of scented wax in the top bowl and a lit candle in the bottom chamber. The scented wax then melts from the heat of the flame, and the scent is spread around the area.
